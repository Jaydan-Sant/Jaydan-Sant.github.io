Who I am

My name is Jaydan Sant. I am currently a student in Computer Games Programming at Staffordshire University and previously studied VR and Unity game development at Halesowen College. As you explore this website you will find information about the various projects I have worked on across my career so far.

What I do

Here are some examples of the things I've made.

Pacman C++ Game (Visual Studio 2019)

A fully functional Pacman game composed in Visual Studio 2019 for Staffordshire University's 'Fundamentals of Games and Graphical Systems Development' module. It is complete with many features such as collectibles, a speed boost mechanic, enemies and audio.

"Glitch in the System" VR Game (Unity Engine)

Developed in Unity Engine for Halesowen College subsidiary Hell's Own Games, Glitch in the System is a VR shooter where the player must fight to reclaim their home from a race of cybernetic alien invaders. The gameplay involves exploring a set map and eliminating all of the enemies within, before exiting the stage. The player will need to react quickly to their surroundings, as their enemy's attacks will need to be avoided with a well-timed dodge or quick attack.

Game Engine Creation Portfolio

A collection of C++ programs developed for Staffordshire University's 'Game Engine Creation' module. These programs demonstrate knowledge and application of various sectors of C++ programming, inlcuding functions, structs, arrays, loops and more.
